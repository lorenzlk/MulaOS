Everything We're Shopping From Nordstrom's Secret Winter Sale

Hurry, these rare deals won't last long

By Elly LeavittPublished: Jan 12, 2025 9:58 PM EST

Nordstrom's Half-Yearly Sale has come and gone, but the retailer is still quietly offering extended discounts across nearly every category—including winter wardrobe essentials, coveted designer bags, and so much more.

So far, we're seeing up to 50 percent off everything from a Max Mara wrap coat and Reformation polo sweater to a trendy Rebecca Minkoff suede tote bag. If you're looking for a new staple pair of sneakers, popular models like the Adidas Samba and Hoka Clifton 9 are currently up to 40 percent off. And to prep your closet for warmer temperatures, we even spotted steep discounts on a few transitional weather staples—like an Alex Mill chore coat and pleated Sandro mini skirt—that you'll be eager to wear on repeat this spring.

To help you narrow down the selection, we've made a list of our favorite fashion finds from Nordstrom's secret winter sale—plus a few standout beauty, home, and travel deals that you won't want to miss, either. Keep scrolling to shop our edit.
