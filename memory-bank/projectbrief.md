# Project Brief: Mula SDK & Operations System

## Overview

Mula is an **AI-powered monetization engine for publishers** that combines content analysis, generative AI, dynamic deployment, personalization, and analytics into a unified workflow.

## Core Mission

Drive **affiliate revenue, ad monetization, and engagement** for digital publishers through intelligent content analysis and automated product placement.

## Project Scope

This repository (MulaOS) serves as the operational backbone for managing:
- Publisher partnerships and relationships
- Internal projects and task management
- Contact and company information
- Migration workflows from Notion to Google Workspace

## Key Components

### 1. Mula SDK (Core Product)
An agent-based system with six specialized AI agents:
- **Weston**: Content analysis & taxonomy
- **Sally**: Generative AI & product discovery
- **Taka**: Dynamic deployment & control
- **Andy**: Analytics & reporting
- **Occy**: Monetization & optimization
- **Cal**: Experimentation & A/B testing

### 2. MulaOS (This Repository)
Operational management system for:
- Partner relationship management
- Project tracking and task management
- Contact database
- Migration workflows and automation scripts

## Current Focus

Transitioning operational data from Notion to Google Workspace while maintaining all relationships and data integrity. This includes:
- Companies (partners and publishers)
- Contacts (both partner contacts and general contacts)
- Projects and tasks
- Activity tracking
- Pipeline management

## Technology Stack

- **Languages**: JavaScript/Node.js, Python
- **Data Sources**: Notion exports (CSV), Google Sheets
- **Tools**: Google Apps Script, custom migration workflows
- **Infrastructure**: Redis (Bull queues), Athena + S3, Postgres, Impact API, Slack

## Goals

1. **Operational Excellence**: Streamlined partner and project management
2. **Data Integrity**: Accurate relationship tracking across all entities
3. **Automation**: Reduce manual work through intelligent workflows
4. **Scalability**: Support growing publisher partnerships efficiently
